BreakOut!

New Game Difficulty: Easy Difficulty: Hard Pause Sound

Goal:

  • Destroy all the blocks to win! Simple.

Instructions:

  • To start a new game, press the N key.
  • Use the left and right arrows to move your paddle.
  • Press the Spacebar to pause the game, or press the S key to toggle the sound.
  • Hint: each end of the paddle causes the ball to gain momentum. Use it to your advantage!
  • Flip onto HARD MODE for an extra challenge. Good luck keeping up.
